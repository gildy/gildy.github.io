Project: Empatchi., 2015

An adhesive, yet affective, electronic patch that shares physical touch and warmth as a novel sense of emotional connection by linking physical sensations in the body. More info: http://interaction.work

ACE 2015

12th International Conference on Advances in Computer Entertainment Technology. http://ace2015.info

Future Scent, 2014

Scent emitting app interface exclusively tailored for Wick and Tallow to promote their luxury candle brand. Featured at London Design Festival 2014.

Mugaritz Virtual Dining, 2014

Collaboration with 2 Michelin-starred restaurant in Spain. The app, along with Scentee device enables user to virtually prepare a broth dish. The device will emit scent as the user finished crushing herbs and spices in a virtual mortar.

RingU, 2014

Interactive ring to send your significant other a remote 'hug' no matter how far you are from each other.

Smoke on The Soda, 2013

Guitar hack + Arduino + Augmented Reality + Rhythm Game + Drink mixer. Not your average Guitar Hero. A new way to play, taste, and “drink” your music.

Kiwwi, 2012

Language Learning Gone Social. Kiwwi matches you with people around based on your preferences and likes and brings you out to start a real world conversation to improve your language skill.

iMeishi, 2010

Online business card management system. The system extracts text from scanned card images, put it into the database, and show the information to the users online.
